Terms and Conditions

  • At the Lessons: Please arrive for your lesson in a timely manner. Most of our studios offer a waiting area where parents can sit during the lesson. Due to scheduling constraints, lesson times will not be extended if arriving late.
  • Tuition: Students have the option of paying for casual or ongoing lessons. Ongoing lessons are taught on a weekly or fortnightly basis and require a minimum commitment of one term. Payment for ongoing lessons is due in full 7 days prior to the start of the term. We accept payment by cash, credit card or electronic funds transfer. Tuition fees are non-refundable.
  • Mid-term enrolments: Are possible dependent on availability and are billed for the remaining weeks in the term. Casual lessons may be booked dependent on availability and are billed at the casual rates.
  • Late Payments: When payment is not received by the due date, you will receive a reminder via email or SMS. In the event that payment is more than 14 days overdue, a $20 late payment fee applies. An account keeping fee of $5 + GST per month applies for invoices that are more than 30 days overdue.
  • Cancellations / Missed Lessons: If the teacher needs to change or cancel a lesson, that lesson will be rescheduled, credited or refunded. In some cases, a substitute teacher may be provided if a teacher is unavailable due to illness.
    If a student cancels a lesson, make-up lessons are available if adequate notice was given, dependent on teacher availability. Make-up lessons are limited to once per term and must be used during the term. `
    For all lessons, if less than 24 hours cancellation notice is given prior to the scheduled lesson time, that lesson will be forfeited.
  • Illness: If you are feeling unwell and unable to attend your lesson, please contact your teacher to reschedule the lesson (as above). Parents are advised not to send children to lessons if they are feeling unwell.
  • Materials: The teacher will suggest music books and resources which can be purchased through the studio, or at your local music store. Please bring a notebook to all lessons for the teacher to record lesson notes and homework.
  • School & Public Holidays: Piano lessons are available during the holidays and attendance is optional for ongoing students.
