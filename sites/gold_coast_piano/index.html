Gold Coast Piano Lessons

Gold Coast Piano is the premier place for piano lessons, and with good reason.
We started over 20 years ago and cracked the code to making lessons fun and engaging for all ages. Our curriculum and unique musical games have been crafted to meet the needs of every student. Our piano teachers have years of experience teaching music in the Gold Coast area, and know how to motivate students of any skill level to play piano.

Don’t worry, you won’t need to be reminded to practice. We do all the work for you!
Our goal is to instill confidence in students so they believe in themselves and can take the stage. This confidence and sense of community prepares students for their future.

Free First Lesson

Gold Coast Piano has a goal to fit in with your schedule.
We value a great teacher/student match and see it as a key to success for all students. You can arrange a trial lesson with one of the best piano teachers the Gold Coast has to offer. Please submit the booking form and we will contact you as soon as possible to arrange your first free lesson.
Our teachers come to you, whether that’s physically in your home, or online. In both cases, you get all the benefits of top-class teachers in the comfort of your own space. This cuts down on the amount of distractions and is a great way for you to feel comfortable and focused. It also saves you time travelling to and from lessons each week!

Lesson Pricing

We want to make organising piano lessons easy.
Enrolments are open year round, and we offer a pro-rata first term with an easy-to-understand payment structure and make-up lesson policy supported by our outstanding customer service. From $50 for 30 minutes, lessons can be held in the comfort of your own home.
We offer weekly lessons, both in-home and online. Lessons are billed in advance for each term, which run in tandem with the public schools dates. Gold Coast Piano lessons offers engaging piano tuition for contemporary students who want to learn great music.

Concerts

Our Annual Gala Concert is the event of the year!
Our Gala Concerts are a fantastic opportunity for students to perform for family and friends on a grand piano in an intimate concert setting, with light show, big screen projection and performances by our students and teachers. The concert is also a fabulous introduction to performance skills, which build confidence both personally and musically within a supportive environment. During Term 3, teachers will focus on preparing enrolled students for performance by including a stage preparation module into their weekly classes.
